About CHEMAG Trading

CHEMAG TRADING CORPORATION was formed in 2004 as a trading company of food and beverage ingredients. Our core business is providing complete supply chain solutions to the various Philippine manufacturing industries. and customer segments. We service an extremely diversified group of customers locally and suppliers globally.

We source new products and technologies to introduce into the country. We maintain strong relationships with our industry's top manufacturers and distributors from around the globe. Backed by a management staff that has almost two decades of experience and expertise in import trading, our customers have come to rely on us to locate sources of high quality raw materials and finished products from global suppliers. Aside from our products, Chemag Trading Corporation also offers to its clients continuous consultancy services in nutrition and food technology.
